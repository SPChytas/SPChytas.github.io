Research

My general expertise lies in Machine/Deep Learning, with my main focus, during my PhD studies, being on structure encoding in latent spaces. My goal is to combine ideas from multiple fields (machine learning, algorithms, optimization, differential and abstract algebra) so that I offer new capabilities or improve existing solutions.
